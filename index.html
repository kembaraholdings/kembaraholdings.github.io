The Kembara Story.

Our Humble Beginnings.

Kembara group was established in 2014. Starting with Kembara Sarjana Sdn Bhd in Malaysia. Since then it has grown into a network of more than 10 companies in the group spanning Malaysia, Singapore & Indonesia.


Our business activities include Oil and PetroChemical trading, Wood and Rattan, Power Generation plants and equipment, Mining and Construction.
